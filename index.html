Disclaimer

To keep this hobby safe and in accordance with the current Civil Aviation Authority regulation (CAP393 The Air Navigation Order 2016), we request our members to:

  • Always perform a pre-flight check and verify that the quadcopter can fly properly.
  • Do not fly over congestioned area and less than 50 meters than people, buildings or any objects.
  • Before powering up your FPV Quad please check the laminated "Pilots VTX frequency list" at the field for any spare VTX bands - please be familiar with changing the frequency of your VTX/receiver. We'll allocate frequencies at the field before flying and if there are enough pilots you'll be paired with a channel buddy. Preferably your VTX will be adjustable to transmit at 25mW. This VTX FT48X is a good option... or if you want more features such as channel changing from your OSD look at something like the TBS unify pro.
  • Always call out your intention to "power-up" your quad to other members flying and be ready to power down due to VTX clashes, please get some distance from other active pilots and change frequencies or wait until they've landed and power up and enjoy your flight. Pilots not using 5.8ghz video systems may power up and fly at will :)
  • Always call it loud for active fliers when your picking up your downed craft on the track, some members fly with a narrow lens and may not see you.
  • If you're in a group when flying please be expedient about taking off when powered and ready to fly (best to power the quad last) up and disconnect power from the quads when landing as not to over-power other pilots VTX video feeds with RF bleed over and harmonics.
